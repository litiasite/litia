    Litia

    Litia was created to curb the spread of eco-anxiety: a feeling of anxiety generated by awareness of the ongoing environmental crisis and climate change, which manifests itself especially among those who are fighting against it. Litia believes that in order to alleviate rising levels of climate anxiety, it is necessary to look at climate change events from a different perspective: that of rocks. Studies and research based on thousands of years of knowledge of rocks show that climate change has always been present and always will be. has always been present and always will be; that our biocentric view is limited; and that ecological anxiety is a meaning ecological anxiety is a meaningless feeling. To do this, Litia created Specta.

    Specta

    Specta is the device that protects against eco-anxiety by silencing the screens that show harmful visual materials on climate change and which leads to seeing them from the another perspective. Through the language of the Rocks, Specta shows the same events, apparently catatrophic, as relaxing stratifications that dilate the user’s perception in a space of dialogue with the Rocks. This new antibiocentric perspective leads us to look at climate change as such: a normal cycle that cannot be stopped.
